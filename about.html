About Myself


Hello Everyone, My Name is James Wilson, and Welcome to my Photography Website, I am an Urban and Landscape Photographer based out of Bloomington, Indiana.


Throughout my lifetime, I enjoy traveling to differeent cities and landscapes across the Eastern and Midwestern parts of the United States. Enjoying the beauty of the environment and surrounding that these places have to offer. However, my time in these places is limited and do not necessarily enjoy the scenery as much as I would like to. Therefore, I take photographs of the most important places I have visited so that I can both take the places I have experiences with me in memory and share them with the people I care for the most.


So join in and explore the different adventures I have and am currently taking and you will find that the photographs taken mean more than what has been taken from the lense.
